A bit about our History

Established in 2001, Sichuan Restaurant was initially under the name of Grand Ruby. The restaurant served Cantonese food and Dim Sum. The restaurant then changed its name to Shanghai Restaurant in 2005, serving Japanese, Cantonese and Shanghai dishes. Another 4 years later, in 2009, the restaurant changed to serve Sichuan cuisine, under the current name of Sichuan Restaurant.
Why not try one our speciality hotpots? All of the ingredients in the hotpot are made to order and are available for groups. Our restaurant provides flavoursome dishes and an overall fantastic ambiance. We also have six karaoke rooms available for hire. Book your party now!
